Key Services

Helicopter Electromagnetic (EM) Surveys

Detecting conductive ore bodies with pinpoint accuracy.

The helicopter EM survey uses a high-power transmitter and high-frequency signals for deep penetration, detecting sulfide deposits and enabling deeper exploration of weakly conductive targets.
Magnetic Surveys

Mapping geological structures to reveal mineral-rich zones.

Magnetic surveys identify variations in Earth's magnetic field to locate faults and folds, aiding in the study of subsurface geology and detecting iron ore, nickel, and gold deposits.

Radiometric Surveys

Measuring surface radioactivity to assist in mineral targeting.

Radiometric surveys measure potassium and uranium, detect variations in gamma radiation, and help identify uranium, rare earth elements, and other mineral-rich zones.
